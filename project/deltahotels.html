Delta Hotels by Mariott

Delta Hotels is a luxurious, four-star hotel and resort chain set out to deliver excellence for their customers. Primarily located across Canada, Delta Hotels is now part of the Mariott International group.

PROJECT OVERVIEW
They reached out for a new in-room dining menu.
PROJECT GOALS
The goal is to generate a clear, simple menu for their customers, laying out all the content but differentiating each item option without having to resort to using bullet points.
FINAL RESULTS
Clean and fitting to company brands
